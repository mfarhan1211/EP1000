Parametric Design

Parametric design is a process based on algorithmic thinking that enables the expression of parameters and rules that, together, define, encode and clarify the relationship between design intent and design response.

Parametric design has made it very easy for me to change dimensions of my object without needing to start over.

Parametric design is very simple as it only requires you to set the dimensions of your design to the assigned parameters.
